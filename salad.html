Salad Recipe

Salad

Begin by washing and drying all your vegetables thoroughly. Start with a base of mixed greens such as romaine, spinach, and arugula in a large salad bowl. Add in chopped cucumbers, halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced red onions, and diced bell peppers for a colorful and crunchy texture. For added protein, consider including grilled chicken breast slices, chickpeas, or feta cheese crumbles. To enhance the flavor, you can also add olives, avocado slices, or nuts like walnuts or almonds.

For the dressing, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice or balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper in a small bowl until well combined. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving to keep the greens fresh and crisp. Toss everything together gently to ensure all ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing. Serve immediately as a light meal or a side dish to your favorite entr√©e.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (romaine, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • Grilled chicken breast slices (optional)
  • Chickpeas (optional)
  • Feta cheese crumbles (optional)
  • Olives (optional)
  • Avocado slices (optional)
  • Nuts (walnuts or almonds) (optional)
  • For the dressing:
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice or balsamic vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine mixed greens, chopped cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, and diced bell pepper.
  3. Add optional ingredients like grilled chicken, chickpeas, feta cheese, olives, avocado slices, or nuts if desired.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice or balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving.
  6. Toss everything together gently to ensure all ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.
  7. Serve immediately as a light meal or a side dish.
