Pizza Recipe

Pizza

Start by making the pizza dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture and stir until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.

Once the dough has risen, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, and add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top and sprinkle with dried oregano and basil.

Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)
  • Dried oregano and basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions. Roll out each portion on a floured surface.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  10. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle with dried oregano and basil (optional).
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  12. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve hot.
